Privacy Policy - BillKeep

Last updated: March 24, 2026

BillKeep is developed by Citadel Labs. This policy explains how we handle your data.

Data We Collect

BillKeep stores all bill data locally on your device and optionally syncs via your personal iCloud account. We do not maintain any servers or databases that store your personal information.

Receipt Scanning

BillKeep offers an optional AI-powered receipt scanning feature. When you use this feature, a photo of your receipt is sent to Anthropic's Claude API for processing. The image is used solely to extract bill details (payee, amount, due date) and is not stored by Citadel Labs. Anthropic's data handling is governed by their own privacy policy. No receipt data is retained after processing.

iCloud Sync

If you enable iCloud, your data is stored in your personal iCloud account and governed by Apple's Privacy Policy. Citadel Labs has no access to this data.

Subscriptions

BillKeep offers optional auto-renewable subscriptions processed by Apple via StoreKit. We do not collect or store any payment information.

Analytics and Tracking

BillKeep does not use any analytics frameworks, advertising SDKs, or third-party tracking tools.

Third-Party Services

The only third-party service BillKeep communicates with is Anthropic's Claude API, used exclusively for the optional receipt scanning feature. No other third-party services are integrated.

Children's Privacy

BillKeep does not knowingly collect data from children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.

Contact

If you have questions about this policy, contact us at support@citadellabs.app.
