On Privacy

Your data is yours. The apps are built so we never see it.

Most financial apps route your data through their servers — for analytics, for insights, for training their models. Citadel Labs doesn't do that. Your bills and subscriptions live on your iPhone and sync through your own iCloud account. We don't keep a database of it. We couldn't hand it over if anyone asked.

No analytics

No Mixpanel, no Amplitude, no Firebase. We don't watch how you use the app. If something breaks, you email us.

No ads inside

You paid for the app, or you're on a free tier. Either way, you're not the product.

No server database

Your data lives on your device and your iCloud. We don't store a copy anywhere.

No dark patterns

One tap to cancel a subscription. Paywalls that say what you're paying for. No "are you sure?" loops.
