Privacy Policy - Rebill

Last updated: April 20, 2026

Rebill is developed by Citadel Labs. This policy explains how Rebill handles your data.

Summary

Rebill is designed to keep your subscription data on your own devices. We do not operate servers that store your financial information, we do not sell data, and we do not use analytics, advertising SDKs, or third-party trackers.

Data Stored on Your Device

All subscription information you enter — merchant names, amounts, renewal dates, categories, notes, and price history — is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework.

iCloud Sync

If you are signed into iCloud, Rebill automatically syncs your subscription data through your personal iCloud account using Apple's CloudKit. This data is stored under your Apple ID in Apple's iCloud infrastructure, not on any server operated by Citadel Labs. We cannot access, read, or export your iCloud-stored data.

iCloud usage is governed by Apple's Privacy Policy. You can disable iCloud sync for Rebill anytime in iOS Settings → [your name] → iCloud → Apps Using iCloud.

In-App Purchases

Rebill offers optional in-app purchases processed by Apple through StoreKit. Citadel Labs never sees or stores your payment information — all transactions are handled directly by Apple. We receive only anonymous purchase receipts necessary to unlock features you've paid for.

Analytics and Tracking

Rebill does not use any analytics SDKs, advertising SDKs, or third-party tracking tools. We do not track which features you use, how long you spend in the app, or any behavioral data.

Third-Party Services

Rebill does not integrate with or send your data to any third-party services, with the exception of Apple's iCloud and StoreKit as described above.

Children's Privacy

Rebill is not directed at children under the age of 13 and does not knowingly collect data from children.

Data Retention and Deletion

Since Rebill does not store your data on our servers, there is no server-side deletion to request. To delete your data:

  • From your device: delete the app, or remove entries within the app
  • From iCloud: iOS Settings → [your name] → iCloud → Manage Account Storage → Rebill → Delete Data

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.

Contact

If you have questions about this policy, contact us at support@citadellabs.app.
