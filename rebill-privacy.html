Privacy Policy - Rebill

Last updated: March 24, 2026

Rebill is developed by Citadel Labs. This policy explains how we handle your data.

Data We Collect

Rebill does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. All subscription data you enter is stored locally on your device and optionally synced via your personal iCloud account.

iCloud Sync

If you enable iCloud, your data is stored in your personal iCloud account and governed by Apple's Privacy Policy. Citadel Labs has no access to this data.

In-App Purchases

Rebill offers optional one-time purchases processed by Apple via StoreKit. We do not collect or store any payment information.

Analytics and Tracking

Rebill does not use any analytics frameworks, advertising SDKs, or third-party tracking tools.

Third-Party Services

Rebill does not integrate with or send data to any third-party services.

Children's Privacy

Rebill does not knowingly collect data from children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
